SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 51
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  Barbara Tolleson
 
         WHEREAS, A full and generous life drew to a close with the
  passing of Barbara Harris Tolleson of Longview on June 8, 2025,
  at the age of 87; and
         WHEREAS, Born in Panola County on April 22, 1938, to Grady
  Harris and Syble Pitts Harris, the former Barbara Harris attended
  school in Tenaha and Houston before her family moved to Longview
  in 1950; after graduating from Longview High School in 1955, she
  enrolled at the BMI Business Institute and worked as a secretary;
  she later rose to become a top salesperson at JCPenney, and she
  served as a beauty consultant with BeautiControl cosmetics; and
         WHEREAS, In all her endeavors, Mrs. Tolleson enjoyed the
  love and support of her husband, Joe, with whom she shared a
  rewarding marriage that spanned 46 years; she took great pride in
  her son, Kenneth, and her daughter, Kimberly, as well as her
  stepsons, John and Joe, and her treasured family eventually grew
  to include 11 grandchildren and 15 great-grandchildren; and
         WHEREAS, A woman of profound faith, Mrs. Tolleson was a
  longtime member of Mobberly Baptist Church; she took pleasure in
  attending the school events of her grandchildren and such hobbies
  as home decorating, traveling, cooking, and crocheting; and
         WHEREAS, Barbara Tolleson led a life of caring devotion to
  her loved ones, and although she is deeply missed, her spirit
  will forever live in their hearts; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory
  of Barbara Harris Tolleson and extend sincere sympathy to the
  members of her family: to her husband, Joe V. Tolleson; to her
  children, Kenneth Taylor and Kimberly Taylor Davis and her
  husband, Jamie; to her stepsons, John D. Tolleson and his wife,
  Ginette, and Joe D. Tolleson and his wife, Susan; to her
  sister-in-law, Judy Stanley and her husband, Larry; to her
  grandchildren, Karrie, Ryan, and Nicole Taylor, Taylor Gray,
  Allison Gentry, DeAnna and Chris Davis, and Trent, Trey, Kate,
  and Jack Tolleson; to her great-grandchildren; and to her other
  relatives and friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of Barbara Tolleson.
 
  Hughes
