SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 40
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  Thelma Snedeker
 
         WHEREAS, Family and friends will forever cherish their
  memories of Thelma McCuller Snedeker of Longview, who passed away
  on January 6, 2025, at the age of 87; and
         WHEREAS, The daughter of Haskell Mahan and Willa Josephine
  Nash Mahan, the former Thelma Mahan was born on January 26, 1937,
  in Pawhuska, Oklahoma, and grew up sharing in the companionship
  of her twin sister, Velma, and sister, Margie; she attended
  school in the Pine Tree Independent School District and later
  continued her education at Kilgore College; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Snedeker entered the workforce as a teenager
  when she took a job at a Longview soda shop; she went on to work in
  the meat department at a number of grocery stores before
  embarking on a rewarding career as a caregiver in East Texas that
  spanned more than three decades; and
         WHEREAS, This beloved woman married Robert McCuller in
  1956, and the couple shared a fulfilling union of nearly six
  decades until Mr. McCuller's passing; they were blessed with
  seven children, Robin, Rena, Rhonda, Roger, Richard, Robert, and
  Randy, and through the years, Mrs. Snedeker had the privilege of
  seeing her family grow to include nine grandchildren, Matthew,
  Courtney, Koda, Miranda, Monica, Tyler, Logan, Roger, and
  Reagan, as well as numerous great-grandchildren; her life was
  further enriched by her marriage to Walter Snedeker, who also
  preceded her in death; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Snedeker enjoyed a variety of hobbies,
  including dancing, fishing, and traveling, and she delighted in
  playing card games and dominoes with her friends; in all her
  endeavors, she drew great strength from her Baptist faith; and
         WHEREAS, Although Thelma Snedeker is deeply missed, those
  who held her dear will always remember the warmth and kindness
  she brought into their lives; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory
  of Thelma McCuller Snedeker and extend heartfelt sympathy to the
  members of her family: to her daughters, Robin McCuller, Rena
  Fields, and Rhonda Glezen and her husband, Scott; to her sons,
  Roger McCuller Sr. and Richard McCuller; to her grandchildren,
  Matthew Comer, Courtney and Koda Fields, Miranda McCuller,
  Monica McCuller, Tyler and Logan Glezen, Roger McCuller Jr., and
  Reagan McCuller; to her sister, Margie Radford; and to her other
  relatives and friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of Thelma Snedeker.
 
  Hughes
