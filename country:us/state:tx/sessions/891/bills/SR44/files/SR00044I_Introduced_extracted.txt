89(1) SR 44 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S10251 HMR-D By: Hughes S.R. No. 44 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, A full and generous life drew to a close with the passing of Barbara Ruth Mize Abbott of Longview on April 9, 2025, at the age of 77; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Thomas and Alice Willis, the former Barbara Willis was born in Lindale on February 12, 1948, and she grew up in the Wood County area with the companionship of eight siblings, Frank, Jessie, Marie, Joe, Earnest, Odessa, Joyce Ann, and Dale; and WHEREAS, She married K. T. Mize Jr. on October 9, 1965, and the couple shared a fulfilling union that spanned 42 years until his passing in 2008; fortunate enough to find love again, she wed Frank Abbott Sr. on May 21, 2011; she took great pride in her four children, Tony, Corey, Keenan, and Jennifer, and her three stepsons, David, Preston, and Frank, and through the years, she had the pleasure of seeing her treasured family grow to include 11 grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren; she cherished the time she spent with her loved ones above all else; and WHEREAS, In 1982, Mrs. Abbott cofounded Longview Lawn & Garden Equipment with her first husband, and two of her sons have since assumed its ownership; a woman of strong faith, she was a valued member of Saint Andrew Presbyterian Church; in her leisure hours, she enjoyed hosting game nights, baking, fishing, and camping, and she also delighted in bird-watching and tending to her garden; and WHEREAS, Although she is deeply missed, the passage of time will never diminish the joy and love that Barbara Abbott brought into the lives of all who knew her; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Barbara Ruth Mize Abbott and extend sincere condolences to the members of her family: to her husband, Frank Abbott Sr.; to her children, Tony Mize and his wife, Lisa, Corey Mize and his wife, Beverly, Keenan Mize and his wife, Traci, and Jennifer Johnson and her husband, David; to her stepsons, David Abbott and his wife, Ann, Preston Abbott and his wife, Susan, and Frank Abbott Jr. and his wife, Susan; to her siblings, Odessa Willis Belcher, Joyce Ann Willis Bullard, and Dale Willis and his wife, Patsy; to her grandchildren, Kimberly Mize Williams and her husband, Alex, Kristina Mize Dorsey and her husband, Rickey, Kacie Mize Spurr and her husband, Robbie, Dakota Mize and his wife, Mariana, Montana Mize and his wife, Shaynee, Nathan Mize and his wife, Tori, Lauren Mize, Tye Johnson, Kayla Bradshaw, Casey Gunn, and Madison Schreiber; to her great-grandchildren; and to her other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Barbara Abbott.
