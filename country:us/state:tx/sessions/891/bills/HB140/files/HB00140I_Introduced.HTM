A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the repeal of a prohibition on property owners'
  association rental restrictions based on a person's method of
  payment.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 202.024, Property Code, is repealed.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
