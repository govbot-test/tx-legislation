A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to state preemption of regulations adopted by a political
  subdivision of this state relating to assisted living facility
  emergency preparedness and contingency operations plans.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 247.073(j), Health and Safety Code, as
  added by H.B. 3595, Acts of the 89th Legislature, Regular Session,
  2025, and effective September 1, 2025, is repealed.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
