SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 95
         WHEREAS, Dr. James L. Grigsby Jr. retired on
  August 8, 2025, concluding an exemplary career in the field of
  dentistry that spanned more than five and a half decades; and
 
         WHEREAS, A proud graduate of Baylor College of Dentistry,
  Dr. Grigsby practiced in Richardson, Garland, and Longview,
  bringing both cutting-edge skill and heartfelt care to every
  patient; he additionally helped to pioneer dental implant
  procedures; and
 
         WHEREAS, Over the decades, Dr. Grigsby has earned a
  sterling reputation for his unmatched work ethic, his friendly
  demeanor, and his clinical expertise; in all his endeavors, he
  draws strength from his Christian faith; and
 
         WHEREAS, Widely admired for his kindness, integrity, and
  steadfast commitment to excellence, Dr. James Grigsby has
  rendered compassionate service to countless individuals and
  families, and he may indeed reflect with pride on a career well
  spent as he embarks on the next exciting chapter of his life; now,
  therefore, be it
 
         RESOLVED, That Dr. James L. Grigsby Jr. be congratulated on
  his retirement from the field of dentistry and that he be
  extended sincere best wishes for the future.
 
  Paxton
