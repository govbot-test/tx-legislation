SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 10
         WHEREAS, Burnt Bean Co. in Seguin has earned the No. 1
  ranking on the Texas Monthly list of the 50 Best BBQ Joints in
  Texas for 2025, and the receipt of this prestigious accolade
  provides a fitting opportunity to recognize the restaurant's
  founders and pitmasters, Ernest Servantes and David Kirkland;
  and
         WHEREAS, Published every four years, the Texas Monthly
  ratings are considered one of the most definitive assessments of
  the Lone Star State's barbecue establishments; the 2025 list was
  compiled by the publication's barbecue editor, Daniel Vaughn, in
  conjunction with a team of 26 taste testers who visited
  restaurants across Texas; and
         WHEREAS, A self-trained chef, Ernest Servantes won his
  first barbecue cook-off in 2009 and rose to national attention
  with his victory in a 2012 episode of the Food Network show
  Chopped Grill Masters; while on the barbecue circuit,
  Mr. Servantes frequently competed against future Burnt Bean
  Co. co-owner David Kirkland, and the two pitmasters enjoyed a
  friendly rivalry before officially teaming up to win cook-offs
  across the state; and
         WHEREAS, In 2019, this powerhouse duo launched a pop-up
  restaurant, which consistently sold out within an hour of
  opening; despite the pandemic, they took their business to the
  next level, establishing the brick-and-mortar Burnt Bean Co. in
  2020 to wide acclaim; known for its traditional meats, its
  Mexican-inspired sides, and its rotating brunch menu, the eatery
  has garnered praise from The New York Times as well as a 2024
  Michelin Bib Gourmand Award, and Mr. Servantes and Mr. Kirkland
  have been honored multiple times as award semifinalists by the
  James Beard Foundation; and
         WHEREAS, While many distinctive foods are associated with
  the Lone Star State, barbecue holds a special place in our
  culinary heritage, and Burnt Bean Co. has truly elevated this
  iconic fare to new heights; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby congratulate Ernest
  Servantes and David Kirkland on the selection of Burnt Bean
  Co. as the No. 1 restaurant in the Texas Monthly 2025 list of the
  50 Best BBQ Joints in Texas and extend to them sincere best wishes
  for continued success; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That official copies of this Resolution be
  prepared for Mr. Servantes and Mr. Kirkland as an expression of
  high regard from the Texas Senate.
 
  Gutierrez, Alvarado, Birdwell, Johnson
