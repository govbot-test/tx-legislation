89(1) HR 14 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S10493 BK-D By: Luther H.R. No. 14 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Assistant Chief Ronald "Joe" Clapp is retiring from the Denison Police Department on July 31, 2025, concluding nearly 37 years of dedicated service to his community; and WHEREAS, A proud Denison native, Joe Clapp joined the Denison Police Department in October 1988, and he distinguished himself in the roles of patrol officer, sergeant, and lieutenant before being promoted to assistant chief of police in 2003; over the course of his career, he has earned the respect of his fellow law enforcement professionals and area residents alike, and he was honored with the Patrol Officer of the Year award from the local VFW chapter in 1996; and WHEREAS, Chief Clapp has demonstrated a strong commitment to professional development, graduating from the FBI National Academy in 2004, earning his master peace officer certification in 2006, and obtaining an associate's degree in criminal justice technology in 2007; and WHEREAS, Through his outstanding skill, professionalism, and leadership, Joe Clapp has furthered the Denison Police Department's mission of serving and protecting the public, and he may indeed reflect with pride on his many achievements as he embarks on the next exciting chapter of his life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby congratulate Assistant Chief Ronald "Joe" Clapp on his retirement from the Denison Police Department and extend to him sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Chief Clapp as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
