SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 83
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  Mary Eady
 
         WHEREAS, A full and generous life drew to a close with the
  passing of Mary Alice Eady of Longview on June 6, 2025, at the age
  of 81; and
         WHEREAS, The daughter of Obie and Vicki Harrell, the former
  Mary Harrell was born in Columbia, Mississippi, on October 9,
  1943, and she grew up with the companionship of three brothers,
  Bobby, Billy, and Tommy; and
         WHEREAS, She married Charles Jerry Eady, and the couple
  shared a rewarding marriage that spanned 53 years until his
  passing in 2015; she took great pride in their two daughters,
  Kelly and Cindi, and through the years, she had the pleasure of
  seeing her treasured family grow to include four grandchildren
  and a great-grandchild; devoted to her loved ones, she cherished
  the time that she spent with them; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Eady was also a woman of strong faith and an
  active member of her church, where she was a regular volunteer;
  she also enjoyed reading the Bible daily; and
         WHEREAS, Mary Eady brightened the lives of all who knew her
  with her compassion, humor, and caring nature, and those who were
  privileged to share in her love and friendship will always
  remember her with great tenderness and affection; now,
  therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory
  of Mary Alice Eady and extend sincere condolences to the members
  of her family: to her daughters, Kelly Alston and her husband,
  Aaron, and Cindi Hudson and her husband, Rocky; to her brother,
  Tommy Harrell; to her grandchildren, Macey and Atley Alston,
  Colby Hudson and his wife, Sarah, and Ty Hudson; to her
  great-grandchild, Jericho Hudson; to her sisters-in-law,
  Margaret Harrell, Joyce Sclease and her husband, Jon, and Barbara
  Morgan; and to her other relatives and friends; and, be it
  further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of Mary Eady.
 
  Hughes
