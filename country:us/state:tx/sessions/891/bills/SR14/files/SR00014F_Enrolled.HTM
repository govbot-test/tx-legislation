SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 14
         WHEREAS, The Senate of the State of Texas is pleased to
  recognize the Midway High School baseball team in Waco on winning
  the 2025 University Interscholastic League Class 6A Division I
  state championship; and
 
         WHEREAS, The Midway Panthers defeated Atascocita High
  School in a hard-fought 6-2 victory on June 7, 2025, at Dell
  Diamond in Round Rock, and they earned the baseball program's
  second state championship in the more than 50 years of its
  history; and
 
         WHEREAS, These skilled players excelled under the superior
  leadership of Head Coach Eddie Cornblum and Assistant Coaches Ben
  Basden, Patrick Mathis, Chase Cryer, and Ramone Reed; the
  athletes demonstrated great talent and perseverance throughout
  the season, and all associated with the team can take pride in
  their outstanding accomplishment; and
 
         WHEREAS, Through countless hours of hard work and an
  unwavering commitment to excellence, the Midway High School
  baseball team realized their goal of becoming state champions;
  their achievement is a source of great pride to their school,
  including Principal Alison Smith, Associate Principal Dawn
  Griffin, and Athletic Director Brad Shelton, and to their many
  supporters in the community; now, therefore, be it
 
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby commend the players,
  coaches, and staff of the Midway High School baseball team on
  their successful season and congratulate them on winning the 2025
  University Interscholastic League Class 6A Division I state
  baseball championship; and, be it further
 
         RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution be prepared for
  the team as an expression of esteem from the Texas Senate.
 
  Birdwell
