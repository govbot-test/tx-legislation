Section 1:
89(1) SCR 1 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Zaffirini S.C.R. No. 1 SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION WHEREAS, Section 17, Article III, Texas Constitution, provides that neither house of the legislature may adjourn for more than three days without the consent of the other house; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED by the 89th Legislature of the State of Texas, That each house grant the other permission to adjourn for more than three days during the period beginning on Wednesday, July 30, 2025, and ending on Monday, August 4, 2025.
