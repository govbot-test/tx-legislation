R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, All who shared in the life of Linda Walton Semlear of
  Longview were deeply saddened by her death on May 17, 2025, at the
  age of 79; and
         WHEREAS, The daughter of Al and Odessa Walton, the former
  Linda Walton was born on August 25, 1945, in Kilgore, where she grew
  up with her siblings; she graduated from Kilgore High School and
  continued her education at Kilgore College before earning a degree
  at Stephen F. Austin State University; and
         WHEREAS, During her time at the university, she met her
  future husband, Jack Semlear, and their wedding in 1967 marked the
  beginning of a rewarding marriage that spanned 46 years until
  Mr. Semlear's passing; after settling in Longview with her husband,
  Mrs. Semlear worked for Texas Eastman and eventually retired from
  the Pine Tree Independent School District; she further contributed
  to the teaching profession as a member of Delta Kappa Gamma; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Semlear was a devoted mother to her two
  children, Eric and Elaine, and with the passing years, she
  delighted in becoming "Nonnie" to her seven grandchildren, Brayden,
  Allison, Kate, Olivia, Jessica, Clay, and Sara; and
         WHEREAS, While her loss is difficult to bear, Linda Semlear
  left behind numerous friends and loved ones whose lives are far
  richer for having known her, and she will always hold a treasured
  place in their hearts; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st
  Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Linda Walton
  Semlear and extend sincere condolences to the members of her
  family: to her son, Eric Semlear and his wife, Kelly; to her
  daughter, Elaine Hargate; to her grandchildren; and to her other
  relatives and friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this
  day, it do so in memory of Linda Semlear.
