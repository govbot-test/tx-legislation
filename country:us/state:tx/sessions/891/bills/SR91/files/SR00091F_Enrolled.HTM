SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 91
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  Javier Vega Jr.
 
         WHEREAS, Family and friends will forever cherish their
  memories of U.S. Border Patrol Agent Javier Vega Jr., who
  tragically passed away in the line of duty on August 3, 2014, at
  the age of 36; and
         WHEREAS, The son of Javier and Marie Vega, Javier Vega was
  born in La Feria on June 17, 1978, and he grew up with the
  companionship of a brother, Jordy; after graduating from La Feria
  High School, he answered his nation's call to duty and enlisted
  in the U.S. Marine Corps; in 2008, following his military
  service, he embarked on a career in law enforcement when he
  joined the U.S. Border Patrol; taking great pride in his job, he
  worked as a K-9 handler at the Kingsville Station; and
         WHEREAS, In all his endeavors, Agent Vega benefited from
  the love and support of his wife, Andrea Brown Vega, and his three
  sons, Javier, Jiovanni, and Jarod; in his leisure hours, he
  enjoyed fishing and running; above all else, he treasured time
  spent with his loved ones; and
         WHEREAS, Texans owe a debt of gratitude to the men and
  women of law enforcement that the passage of time can never
  diminish, and Agent Vega's commitment to protecting the safety
  and well-being of the public will forever be remembered by all
  those who had the good fortune to know him; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life
  of Javier Vega Jr. and extend heartfelt sympathy to his family,
  friends, and former colleagues; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of Javier Vega Jr.
 
  Middleton
