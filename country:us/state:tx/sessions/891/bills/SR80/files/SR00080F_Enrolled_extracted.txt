89(1) SR 80 - Enrolled version - Bill Text SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 80 In Memory of Emma Raney WHEREAS, A life lived with joy and purpose drew to a close with the passing of Emma Renell Raney of Argo on July 20, 2025, at the age of 100; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Els and Lola Babe Wilbanks, the former "Nell" Wilbanks was born in Argo on March 20, 1925, and she grew up with the companionship of two brothers, Alvin and Jude; and WHEREAS, She married the love of her life, Charles Raney, on December 28, 1944, and the couple went on to share a fulfilling marriage that spanned seven decades until his passing in 2014; Mrs. Raney took great pride in their children, Linda, Dale, Kim, and Carolyn, who preceded her mother in death; with the passing years, she had the privilege of watching her treasured family grow to include 12 grandchildren, Angie, Brian, Jerilyn, Michael, Cheryl, Michelle, Krissy, Matt, Vernon, Chris, Charles, and the late Kasi Hopkins, as well as 21 great-grandchildren; and WHEREAS, A woman of faith, Mrs. Raney was a lifelong member of Argo Missionary Baptist Church; in her leisure time, she enjoyed gardening, cooking, quilting, playing 42, traveling with family, and supporting her grandchildren and great-grandchildren in all their endeavors; and WHEREAS, Admired for her warmth, grace, and kindness, Nell Raney brightened the world for all who knew her, and they will forever hold her close in their hearts; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life of Emma Renell Raney and extend sincere condolences to her relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Nell Raney. Hughes ________________________________ President of the Senate I hereby certify that the above Resolution was adopted by the Senate on August 15, 2025, by a rising vote. ________________________________ Secretary of the Senate ________________________________ Member, Texas Senate
