SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 85
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  Anthony Shumake
 
         WHEREAS, Family and friends were deeply saddened by the
  passing of Anthony Lynn Shumake of De Kalb on May 15, 2025, at the
  age of 62; and
         WHEREAS, The son of Lynn and Wilene Shumake, Tony Shumake
  was born on July 6, 1962, and he grew up with the companionship of
  two siblings, Wade and Jana; in all his endeavors, he benefited
  from the love and support of his wife, Barbara, with whom he
  shared a rewarding marriage that spanned nearly four and a half
  decades; he took great pride in his two daughters, Lacey and
  Kelsey, and he carried with him the memory of his two cherished
  sons, Timothy and Nick, who preceded their father in death; over
  the years, he had the pleasure of welcoming into his family five
  grandchildren, Jalena, Hanna, Livia, Hayden, and Kaylynn; and
         WHEREAS, A man of deep faith, Mr. Shumake was a dedicated
  member and deacon of Pinkham Memorial Missionary Baptist Church;
  he was known for his playful spirit and mischievous sense of
  humor, and he enjoyed entertaining others with his jokes and
  pranks; and
         WHEREAS, Although Tony Shumake is greatly missed, he has
  left his family and friends with many cherished memories, and he
  will forever hold a special place in their hearts; now,
  therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life
  of Anthony Lynn Shumake and extend sincere condolences to the
  members of his family: to his wife, Barbara Shumake; to his
  daughters, Lacey Mullins and her husband, Trey, and Kelsey
  McDermott and her husband, Levi; to his grandchildren, Jalena,
  Livia, and Kaylynn McDermott and Hanna and Hayden Mullins; to his
  brother, Wade Shumake, and his wife, Debbie; to his sister, Jana
  Ford, and her husband, Mike; and to his other relatives and many
  friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of Tony Shumake.
 
  Hughes
