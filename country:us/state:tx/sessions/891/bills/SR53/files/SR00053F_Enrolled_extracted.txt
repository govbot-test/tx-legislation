89(1) SR 53 - Enrolled version - Bill Text SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 53 In Memory of William Dunlap WHEREAS, A rich and purposeful life drew to a close with the passing of William David Dunlap of Longview on June 29, 2025, at the age of 86; and WHEREAS, David Dunlap was born to Phelan and Isabelle Dunlap on March 1, 1939, in Longview and grew up sharing in the companionship of two siblings, Janet and Robert; a 1957 graduate of Longview High School, he went on to attend Kilgore College and Texas Tech University; and WHEREAS, In 1961, Mr. Dunlap married Polly Tyson, and the couple embarked on a richly rewarding union that spanned nearly 64 years; a devoted father to their two daughters, Angela and Becky, Mr. Dunlap led the band booster club, attended countless dances, and often played basketball and Ping-Pong with his girls; with the passing years, he had the privilege of welcoming a grandson, Cole, into the family; and WHEREAS, Mr. Dunlap enjoyed a successful career as a purchasing agent that included tenures with Gibson's Discount Center and R. Lacy Oil Company; an active member of the Longview community, he volunteered with such worthy organizations as Asbury House, Longview Community Ministries, and Habitat for Humanity, and he was a dedicated congregant of First United Methodist Church, where he served as a frequent volunteer and as pitcher for the church softball team; later in life, Mr. Dunlap delighted in fixing up his home on Lake Cherokee, and his hobbies included fly-fishing, playing golf, and bird-watching; and WHEREAS, David Dunlap embraced life to the fullest, and he will forever remain a vibrant presence for those who knew and loved him; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of William David Dunlap and extend sincere condolences to the members of his family: to his wife, Polly Dunlap; to his daughters, Angela Martin and her husband, Tony, and Becky Dennis and her husband, Gary; to his grandson, Cole Dennis; and to his many other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of David Dunlap. Hughes ________________________________ President of the Senate I hereby certify that the above Resolution was adopted by the Senate on August 15, 2025, by a rising vote. ________________________________ Secretary of the Senate ________________________________ Member, Texas Senate
