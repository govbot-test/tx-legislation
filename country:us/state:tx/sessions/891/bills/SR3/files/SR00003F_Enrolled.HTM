SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 3
         WHEREAS, For 125 years, the International Alliance of
  Theatrical Stage Employees, Local 76, has played a crucial role
  in supporting and advocating for the rights of skilled laborers
  in San Antonio's entertainment industry; and
         WHEREAS, Founded in 1893, the International Alliance of
  Theatrical Stage Employees (IATSE) represents behind-the-scenes
  professionals, including technicians, designers, and stagehands,
  in a broad spectrum of trades; the alliance encompasses more than
  340 local unions and over 168,000 workers, who bring their
  invaluable technical expertise to bear in countless stage, film,
  and television productions as well as trade shows and exhibitions
  across the United States and Canada; and
         WHEREAS, Driven by their pride in their craft and their
  commitment to fairness and dignity in the workplace, the members
  of IATSE Local 76 have built and sustained the success of their
  chapter for well over a century; the chapter's service area
  includes such celebrated local cultural venues as the Joci Straus
  Performing Arts Center, the Tobin Center for the Performing Arts,
  the AT&T Center, and the Witte Museum, and its support for its
  members extends to an array of payroll, organizational, and
  online resources; and
         WHEREAS, IATSE Local 76 has served as a vital force for
  equity in entertainment, giving voice and power to the
  often-invisible professionals who bring productions to life in
  the Alamo City, and the chapter and its hardworking members are
  indeed worthy of recognition on this special milestone; now,
  therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby honor the International
  Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees, Local 76, on its 125th
  anniversary and extend to all those affiliated with the chapter
  sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for IATSE Local 76 as an expression of high regard from
  the Texas Senate.
 
  Menéndez
