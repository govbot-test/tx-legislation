R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Family and friends were deeply saddened by the
  passing of Kyle Richard Bough of Kilgore on June 14, 2025, at the
  age of 90; and
         WHEREAS, The son of Clyde Earl Bough and Mabel Bough, Kyle
  Bough was born on January 2, 1935, in Stockton, Missouri, where he
  grew up with the companionship of three siblings, Lyle, Donald, and
  Jean Carol; answering his nation's call to duty, he served with the
  U.S. Army for two years; and
         WHEREAS, In 1961, Mr. Bough exchanged vows with his wife,
  Elaine, and the couple shared a rewarding marriage that spanned
  nearly six and a half decades; they were blessed with five children,
  Sheila, Richard, Carol, Michelle, and Scott, and through the years,
  their cherished family grew to include 15 grandchildren, 18
  great-grandchildren, and a great-great-grandchild; and
         WHEREAS, Over the course of his career, Mr. Bough worked as a
  charter bus driver and in the trucking industry before joining the
  Western Company of North America; in 1978, he and his family settled
  in Kilgore; and
         WHEREAS, Although Kyle Bough is greatly missed, he has left
  his loved ones with a wealth of memories that they will forever
  treasure; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st
  Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life of Kyle Richard Bough
  and extend sincere condolences to the members of his family: to his
  wife, Elaine Bough; to his children, Sheila Smithwick and her
  husband, Carl, Richard Bough, Carol Loizeaux and her husband, Tim,
  Michelle Renaud and her husband, Joe, and Scott Bough and his wife,
  Tosha; to his grandchildren, great-grandchildren, and
  great-great-grandchild; and to his other relatives and friends;
  and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this
  day, it do so in memory of Kyle Bough.
