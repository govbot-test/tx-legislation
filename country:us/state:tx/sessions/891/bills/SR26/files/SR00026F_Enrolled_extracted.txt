89(1) SR 26 - Enrolled version - Bill Text SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 26 In Memory of Robert Jones WHEREAS, Family and friends will long cherish their memories of Robert Darrell Jones of Hideaway, who passed away on February 1, 2025, at the age of 90; and WHEREAS, The son of James Robert Jones and Hallie Mae Hibbitt Jones, Darrell Jones was born on December 14, 1934, in Mesquite; following his graduation from high school in 1953, he attended Southern Methodist University; he went on to enjoy a successful career as a sales representative in the furniture and supply industry, and in 1971, he started his own business, Darrell Jones and Associates; and WHEREAS, In all his endeavors, Mr. Jones benefited from the love and support of his wife, the former Mary Lou Pollard, with whom he shared a fulfilling marriage that spanned nearly seven decades; he took great pride in their daughters, Luanne and Jennifer, and with the passing years, he had the pleasure of welcoming into his family four grandchildren and five great-grandchildren; and WHEREAS, A man of strong faith, Mr. Jones was involved in his church; he also served as a Mason and a Shriner, dedicating his time to transporting children in need of medical care to hospitals; during his leisure hours, he enjoyed traveling with his wife in their motor home throughout the United States and Canada; and WHEREAS, Admired for his kindness, his wisdom, and his devotion to his family, Darrell Jones lived a full and generous life, and he will forever hold a special place in the hearts of those he has left behind; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Robert Darrell Jones and extend sincere sympathy to the members of his family: to his wife, Mary Lou Jones; to his daughters, Luanne Fassett and her husband, Byron, and Jennifer Grounds and her husband, Ron; to his grandchildren, Mallory Rix and her husband, Jason, Brayden Fassett and his wife, Louisa, Ryan Grounds, and Jordan Grounds; to his great-grandchildren, Savannah Barkheimer, Jaxson Grounds, Myles Grounds, Samuel Fassett, and Benjamin Fassett; to his sister-in-law, Myra Low, and her husband, Larry; and to his other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Darrell Jones. Hughes ________________________________ President of the Senate I hereby certify that the above Resolution was adopted by the Senate on August 15, 2025, by a rising vote. ________________________________ Secretary of the Senate ________________________________ Member, Texas Senate
