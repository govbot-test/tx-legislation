89(1) HR 12 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S10375 DSH-D By: Villalobos H.R. No. 12 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Corpus Christi native Justin Lamkin of Texas A&M University was selected by the Kansas City Royals in the 2025 Major League Baseball Draft; and WHEREAS, The Royals used the 71st overall pick to take Mr. Lamkin in the second round of the MLB Draft, which was held on July 13 and 14 in Cumberland, Georgia; and WHEREAS, The son of Jeff and Julie Lamkin, Mr. Lamkin first gained acclaim at Calallen High School in Corpus Christi, where he was ranked as the No. 5 left-handed pitcher in the Lone Star State; Texas A&M signed him to its 2022 recruiting class, and as a true freshman, he took the mound as a starter and reliever, tallying a 3-3 record and one save; after an exceptional performance in the postseason, he was named to the 2023 Southeastern Conference All-Tournament Team; continuing to excel in his sophomore season, he helped lead the Aggies to the College World Series, where he allowed just three runs in 10.2 innings; in 2025, he turned in his strongest year yet, finishing with a 3.42 ERA and a team-best 98 strikeouts; and WHEREAS, The highlight of Mr. Lamkin's college career came in May 2025 against Georgia when he tossed his first complete-game shutout, striking out a career-high 15 batters and limiting the Bulldogs to only three hits; as a result, he was named the National Pitcher of the Week by the College Baseball Foundation, the National Collegiate Baseball Writers Association, and Perfect Game; he was also chosen as the SEC Pitcher of the Week, his second time to earn the honor; in March 2025, he became the 22nd member of the 200-K Club at Texas A&M; and WHEREAS, Justin Lamkin's accomplishments on the diamond have furthered Texas' proud legacy of athletic achievement, and this elite pitcher is poised for an outstanding career in professional baseball; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby congratulate Justin Lamkin on his selection in the 2025 MLB Draft and extend to him sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mr. Lamkin as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
