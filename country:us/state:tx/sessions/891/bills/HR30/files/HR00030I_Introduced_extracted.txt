89(1) HR 30 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S10626 DSH-D By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 30 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The first annual West Texas BBQ Cook-Off is taking place in El Paso on October 17 and 18, 2025; and WHEREAS, This much-anticipated event is sanctioned by the International Barbeque Cookers Association as one of the organization's state championship cook-offs; and WHEREAS, The contest draws accomplished pitmasters from across Texas who strive to create the tastiest entries in several different categories; guests have the opportunity to sample a variety of savory dishes while enjoying a fun, family-oriented atmosphere and spirited competition; and WHEREAS, The first annual West Texas BBQ Cook-Off is a fitting celebration of the smoked delicacies that are such an integral part of Texas culture, and it promises to be a source of fond memories for all who attend; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby commemorate the first annual West Texas BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the organizers and participants sincere best wishes for a fun and successful event.
