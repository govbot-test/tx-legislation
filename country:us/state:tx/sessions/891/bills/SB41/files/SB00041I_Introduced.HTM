A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to floodlight and evacuation notice requirements for youth
  camp flood safety.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Chapter 141, Health and Safety Code, is amended
  by adding Section 141.0093 to read as follows:
         Sec. 141.0093.  FLOOD SAFETY REQUIREMENTS. The youth camp
  operator of a youth camp located on or near a body of water that is
  at risk of flooding must:
               (1)  install and maintain floodlights to guide campers
  to safety during an emergency; and
               (2)  for each cabin at the youth camp, post in a
  conspicuous location at or near the entrance of the cabin a sign
  informing campers of the procedures for safely evacuating in the
  event of flooding.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
